Desciption:

EduHub is a free website for teachers and stude nts to help organize their work and destress. Especially during higher grades, due to lots of homework, studies and pressure from parents people mental stress skyrockets. Due to this people perform even worse in upcoming examinations increasing their stress. In such stressful scenarios people resort to vices such as underage smoking and drinking. There is a similar effect for teachers, after an exhausting day of teaching and maintaining classroom decorum, and since it is easier for them to access such goods which affects health. The purpose of this website is too not only improve mental health but organize both students and teachers work to reduce stress and frustration.
